HYUNDAI Elantra 2024

About Car:

The 2024 Hyundai Elantra HD is one of the most famous Hyundai cars offered in Egypt, and belongs to the sedan category. Here are the new specifications and competitive prices in Egypt The 2024 Hyundai Elantra HD appears in four different categories of equipment, and the equipment of each category differs from the other, allowing buyers to choose the category that suits their needs and personal preferences.

  • The car is equipped with a 1600 cc, 4-cylinder engine that generates 121 horsepower.
  • It has a torque of 156 Newton metres, and is linked to a 4-speed automatic transmission.
  • Operates with front traction system.
  • Hyundai Elantra HD reaches a top speed of 182 km/h.
  • The car takes 11.3 seconds to reach from zero to 100 km/h.
  • The fuel consumption of the car is about 6.6 liters per 100 km.
