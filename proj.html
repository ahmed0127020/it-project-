About

Hyundai Motors has served as a pioneer in the automobile industry in Korea since its inception, achieving this level of development through its exclusive technologies. Hyundai Motors then gained fame as a global leader in the automotive industry, exporting cars bearing its brand to more than 200 countries. It also has production bases all over the world. The company is working to expand the automotive market through the large-scale production of hydrogen-powered cars for the first time in the world and the launch of GENESIS, a luxury brand, in addition to working to advance the future of the mobility industry towards a better future based on its leadership in innovation in connectivity and autonomous driving technologies. It also seeks to find better solutions to serve humanity by providing technical innovations aimed at “developing humanity.”
